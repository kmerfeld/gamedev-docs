End Screen / Death Screen

Player view

  When the player dies or when they reach the end goal, they will get
  a screen that pops up showing them their final score and time. The 
  player will also have 2 buttons that they can press, the 'Exit' button
  and the 'Play Again' button. The Exit button will exit the game and the
  Play Again button will start a new game and reset their score and time.

Game Dev's View

  A method to display the end screen will be called when the player's
  health drops to 0 or when the player reaches the goal. When the 
  end screen is constructed it will take the player's score and the
  time it took them and display them on the screen. There will be 2 
  pressable buttons, the 'Exit' button and the 'Play Again' button.
  Pressing the Exit button will call the System to End the program
  and the Play Again button will create a new instance of the scene
  and reset the score and time.
