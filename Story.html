The story of the game (overall narrative)

The goal is to move to the end of the level so the player can steal the lepricaun's gold.

The game is going to be split up into 4 main levels, each will be played in a separate direction (up, down, left, right). We will have various "powerups" to assist the player on the way to the finish of the level.
