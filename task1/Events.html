Events

There will be a number of events that will be triggered by reaching the pot of gold, the player dying, the player hitting a power-up, reaching a boss fight will send the player into a mini-game
  Winning-	Winning will be accomplished when the player reaches the pot of gold
		A message will then be displayed showing final time taken and total points
		earned.

  Dying-	When the player dies by getting hit by an enemy (if the player doesn't
		have some sort of power-up that prevents death) a message will be displayed
		showing time taken and total points earned.

  Power-ups-	Power ups will be scattered throughout the levels to assist the player by giving
		some of their stats modifiers.
