Objects

These are the various objects in the game
  The game will have rainbows that the player can jump on.
  They will allow the player to get to some of the collectibles.

  The game will have shamrocks throughout the levels that the player can collect.
  Collecting these will increase the player's score.

  The game will have horseshoes that will act as projectiles. 
  These will go across the screen in the air and try to hit the player.
