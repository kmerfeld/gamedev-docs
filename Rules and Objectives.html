BASICS

The Rules and Objectives of the game
Rules:-
- To attack enemies: Jump on them.
- To avoid damage: Avoid colliding with the enemies.

Power Ups: Several Powerups would be available during the whole game and their types would be updated soon.

Movement Type:- Walk, Run, Duck and Jump.
              - Double Jump, Wall Jump.

Objective :- Survive each level to qualify for the final one.
           - To steal gold.
